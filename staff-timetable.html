Glossary / FAQ

What is Student Support?
Student Support is in the form of mentoring, in-class or out-of-class support of students. This is assigned to you by SLT.
What is Whole School Support?
Whole School Support is essentially supporting all staff and all students during unstructured times. To supervise students.
What is On-call / Centre Duties?
Being available on-call for incidents and overseeing general centre operations from the Main Foyer.
