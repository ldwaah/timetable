## Executive summary

Some Facilities Coordinator tasks overlap with the proposed Estates Support Officer role on admin and coordination. The comparison below shows where the job descriptions differ in scope and reporting.

1. **Slide 25** matches Facilities Coordinator to **E&F Support Officer** ("Direct Match"). That is ROH's stated path for the FC, not Support Manager. Without clearer practical detail, that label should not be treated as settled.

2. **The Officer JD still removes material FC work** even with that label: manage to assist on contractor register (see JD comparison); contractor recommendations to escalation to Support Manager; budget work under managers not full Facilities coordination with Financial Controller at ~30-40 invoices/week; no disaster recovery, fire warden register, or photocopier duties; and other removals including a removed technical requirement in the person spec.

3. **Support Manager is not suitable** if offered instead of Coordinator: Slide 15 (line manager of 3 Officers), Slides 17 & 21 (cleaning contract), Slide 24 (pooled interview between two managers, loss of 1 job), Slide 21 (Director keeps all budgets).

4. **Ask for a written offer** covering pay, grade, hours, and full JD before accepting Officer or Manager.

## Conclusion

ROH describe the Officer post as a direct match to the Facilities Coordinator role. Some duties overlap on paper, but overlap does not show how the role would work day to day. Important practical terms were left for the new manager to decide. Without fuller clarification and assessment, the proposed role should not be treated as an automatic direct match.
